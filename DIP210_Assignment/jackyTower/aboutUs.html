Why MHS?

Big cities are always expensive to live in, but even more so for young people who have to live independently, whether they are college students, fresh graduates or job seekers.

Youths in Kuala Lumpur also face similar problems. Therefore, the Kuala Lumpur City Hall (DBKL) has proposed a scheme to help youths to rent accommodation at affordable prices
